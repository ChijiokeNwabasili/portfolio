About Me

How I approach frontend development.

I'm Chijioke Nwabasili, a frontend developer who builds websites for small service and product-based businesses that need clarity, structure, and reliability. I focus on creating interfaces that are easy to understand, easy to use, and consistent across devices.

Many websites lose potential customers because information is hard to find or interactions feel confusing. I approach frontend work with that in mind, paying close attention to layout structure, content flow, and performance so users can move through a site without friction.

I work primarily with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, applying accessibility and responsive design best practices throughout each build. Outside of development, I enjoy listening to music, often looping a single track while working through problems.

If you're looking for a frontend developer who values clarity and reliability, feel free to explore my work or reach out.
