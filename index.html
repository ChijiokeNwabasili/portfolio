About Me

Making websites easier to understand, navigate, and act on.

Most business websites don't have a traffic problem, they have a communication problem. Visitors land on the site but leave because it's unclear, cluttered, or hard to navigate. They don't know what to do next, so they do nothing.

I focus on understanding how users interact with a website before writing code, so every page has a clear purpose and direction.

I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, applying best practices in usability, accessibility, and performance.
