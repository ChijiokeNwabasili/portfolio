About Me

I'm Chijioke Nwabasili, a Frontend Developer based in Nigeria. I build user-focused websites that are clear, fast, and aligned with each brand's goals using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My focus is on creating digital experiences that not only look good but also load quickly, adapt across devices, and feel natural to use. I pay close attention to details that shape how people interact with a site, so it feels as smooth as it looks.

For me, development isn't just about writing code, it's about understanding why something should be built a certain way and communicating those choices clearly with teammates and stakeholders.

I'm always learning and I value collaboration, especially when technical and non-technical people come together to solve real problems. If you're looking for a developer who builds with intent and cares about usability from start to finish, feel free to check out my work and get in touch.

I'm also tutoring beginners in frontend development with Dot Campus , teaching HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals. Teaching helps me improve my communication while deepening my understanding of the core web.
