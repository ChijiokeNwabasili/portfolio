About Me

I'm Chijioke Nwabasili, a Frontend Developer based in Nigeria. I build user-focused websites that are visually clear, fast, and aligned with business goals using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I focus on creating digital experiences that not only look good but also load quickly, adapt across devices, and feel natural to use. I pay close attention to details that shape how people interact with a site, ensuring it feels as smooth as it looks.

For me, development isn't just about writing code, it's about understanding why something should be built a certain way and communicating those choices clearly with teammates and stakeholders.

I'm always learning and I value collaboration, especially when technical and non-technical people come together to solve real problems. If you're looking for a developer who builds with intent and cares about usability from start to finish, feel free to explore my work and get in touch.

I also tutor beginners in frontend development with Dot Campus , teaching HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals. Teaching helps me improve communication while deepening my understanding of the core web.
